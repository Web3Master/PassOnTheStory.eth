Much that once was is lost, for none now live who remember it. You see in the early days The Ethereals had the bizarre custom of assigning names and identities based on a random hash, until chance came and Nick.eth created a new decentralized naming protocol. For the time will come when The Ethereals will shape the fortunes of all...
